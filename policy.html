2. Partnership Program: Lifetime Attribution

We believe in rewarding the people who help us grow our ecosystem.

The "Locked-In" Rule: Once a customer uses your referral code for their first purchase, they are permanently linked to your profile in our database.
  • Recurring Commissions: You earn a 16.67% commission on their initial order and every future order they place, even if they message us later without a code.
  • Commission Calculation & Currency Conversion:
    • Percentage-Based: Commissions are calculated as exactly 16.67% of the total gross sale, regardless of whether the customer chooses a full STACK or a customized service package (e.g., removing branding or ordering UI/UX only).
    • Base Currency: The commission is initially calculated in the currency used by the customer (e.g., NGN, USD).
    • Conversion: If your preferred payout method is in a different currency (e.g., receiving USDT for a Naira-based sale), Emmy STACK01 will perform the conversion based on the prevailing market exchange rate at the time of the payout.
  • Notification System: You will receive automated emails when:
    • A lead is captured via your unique link.
    • An order is completed and your commission is ready.
    • A linked client returns for a new project in the future.
  • Expiration of Commissions: Commissions only stop if you delete your data, if the customer uses a different affiliate's code, or if Emmy STACK01 undergoes a change in ownership.
