FAQs

What is Emmy STACK01?

Emmy STACK01 is a one-stop digital agency specializing in high-performance website development and brand identity. We handle everything from UI/UX design and On-page SEO to the technical essentials—including free hosting via GitHub, domain configuration, and Google indexing. We manage the technical "heavy lifting" so you can focus on growing your business.

Why does Emmy STACK01 charge per page?

We use a per-page pricing model to ensure total cost transparency. Whether you need a high-converting one-page landing site or a multi-page corporate website, you’ll know the exact investment required before we begin. This eliminates hidden fees and allows us to scale our web development services to fit your specific budget.

Are the technical services like hosting and Google indexing really free?

Yes! While other agencies charge monthly maintenance fees, we provide free website hosting via GitHub Pages and complimentary Google search indexing. We also handle your domain name configuration and SSL setup at no extra cost. Our goal is to lower the barrier to entry for your digital presence, saving you significant annual costs on traditional hosting providers.

Why is there a "Built by Emmy STACK01" tag on my site?

We take immense pride in every project we launch. This tag serves as a seal of quality, letting your visitors know your site was built to professional standards. It also allows other businesses to easily find the same high-performance web development and design services that we provided for you.
